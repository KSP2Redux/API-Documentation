    GetFlavorText(string, string, ScienceReportType)

    Get the flavor text localization tag for the specified experiment, research location and report type. We first look for flavor texts that match the situation and science region. If we find none, we fall back to flavor texts that only match the situation. If multiple matching flavor texts are found, one is chosen at random.
